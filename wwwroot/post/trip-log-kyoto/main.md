# Trip Log 01 — Kyoto

Date: 2024-02-03

Landed in Kyoto just before sunset. Walked through Gion’s narrow alleys, then up to Kiyomizu-dera for a city view washed in gold. Warm bowl of ramen, colder wind by the river. Not a bad start.
